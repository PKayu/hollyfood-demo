Our Story

More Than a Burger — It's an Experience

Hollyfood is the creation of Francisco Tobar, who brought his passion for cinema and exceptional food together into one unforgettable dining room. The name itself is an homage to Hollywood — and every inch of the restaurant shows it.

From the life-size Darth Vader statue to the vintage Pac-Man arcade games, from Captain America to Harry Potter, Hollyfood is a love letter to the stories that shaped us — paired with the best beef burger you'll find in Northern Utah.

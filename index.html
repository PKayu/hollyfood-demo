Where Burgers Meet Legend

Step into a universe of flavor at Hollyfood — South Ogden's only superhero-themed burger bar. Life-size Darth Vader guards the door, two vintage Pac-Man machines keep the energy high, and every wall tells a story from the biggest movies and comics ever made.

Owner Francisco Tobar brought this vision to life in April 2024, crafting a place where the food is as epic as the décor. 100/100 health score. Fresh beef. Big flavors. Even bigger heroes.
